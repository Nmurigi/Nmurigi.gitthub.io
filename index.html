Welcome!

What you do you desire most? Where do you see yourself in the next few years? what is stopping you from reaching your destiny? These are just some of the questions many of us are not always able to answer owing to our current situations. But, no one ever has it easy. Not even the richest men and women in the world.

It's about not giving up. It is about taking 100% responsibility of your life. Life will always deals us hard blows and throw us curves. However, people have come out of really tragic, challenging and awful situations and have achieved their dreams and reached their destination. It is time to stop holding the pity parties and awaken with a new desire and hope that will propel you to you destiny.

About Alex

ABOUT ALEX

Alex is a devoted family man and a charismatic motivational speaker with one goal in life – to touch every soul with a message of hope that anything is possible. He inspires the desire for everyone to reach their potential regardless of their circumstances.

A preacher and a life coach, Alex aims to teach the value of integrity and hard work. His vision is to inspire the desire for each member of the Human race to reach destiny in God. He works tirelessly to reach out to as many people as possible with the aim of transforming their lives.

By combining traditional methods and social media to inspire and empower his audience, Alex has been an inspiration to many. He emphasizes on the need for people to believe in themselves regardless of which stage they are in their lives.   

But beyond that, warm smile, friendly demeanor, powerful message and his ability to connect with his audience on a deeper level, prompts people to action. A keynote speaker, his purpose is to inspire the desire to reach destiny. He uses his own experiences to challenge and build others.

Revered by his peers as an iconic speaker, Alex sits on the Advisory Board of the Global Mentorship Networks as Chairman. He is also a founding member of the Wazalendo Trust and a former Governing Council member at ILU (International Leadership University), which he served for 7 years. The Trust whose sole purpose is to improve the welfare of young Kenyans is one of his key projects, which also aims at achieving his vision.   

Alex is also heavily involved in community projects. The Zabibu Centre in Ruiru, a Centre for special needs children (physically challenged, blind, FGM victims) is an institution that is close to his heart. Apart from offering financial support, and encouraging messages to the children, he also sits as a Trustee in Centre’s Board of Trustees.  

Alex is a mentor at the Duolos school of Excellence, which has a Young People Mentorship Program in which Alex counsels young adults of between the ages of 18-20 years.
